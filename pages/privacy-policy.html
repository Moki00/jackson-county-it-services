Privacy Policy

Last Updated: December 24, 2025

At GOKAME Tech Support, we respect your privacy and are committed to protecting the personal information you share with us. This policy explains how we collect and use your data when you visit gokame.com

1. Information We Collect

We only collect information that you voluntarily provide through our contact form, such as:

  • Your Name
  • Email Address
  • Service requirements and messages

2. How We Use Your Information

The information you provide is used solely to:

  • Respond to your technical support or web service inquiries.
  • Schedule onsite support visits within our North Georgia service area.

3. Third-Party Services

We use trusted third-party services to help run our website efficiently:

  • EmailJS: Used to securely deliver your contact form messages to our team.
  • Google Maps: Used to display our service area. Google may collect usage data and IP addresses according to their own privacy standards.

4. Data Security

We take reasonable administrative and technical measures to protect your information. However, please be aware that no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions regarding this policy or wish to have your information removed from our records, please contact us via the contact form, or call (470) 272-0054.
